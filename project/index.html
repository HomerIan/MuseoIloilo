MUSEO ILOILO

A Window To The Past, To Ilonggo Local History And Cultural Heritage

Our Story

Built in 1971 and one if the government-sponsored museum outside Manila. Museo Iloilo is under the ownership and management of Iloilo Cultural Research Foundation, Inc. since 1973 up to the present. Its architectural design was inspired by the Paraw sailboat. It is the repository of Western Visayas Heritage. Museo Iloilo has nine different sections: Gallery I and Gallery II, Archeological Section, Weaving Section, World Section, and Cultural Communities Section. Museo Iloilo conducts monthly painting exhibit, annual Summer Art Workshop, and On-the-spot Painting Contest held annually during the celevration of the Iloilo Dinagyang Festival.
